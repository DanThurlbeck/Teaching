About us

Hello, welcome to Pangea We are here to help your child learn a new language through the magic of reading. Reading is one of the best ways to become familiar with a new language. What's more, it's fun! Students can choose the types of books they are interested in, and we will select a suitable one for them at their reading level. We supply native English speakers to guide your child and answer any questions about new vocabulary, grammar or anything else they are curious about.
